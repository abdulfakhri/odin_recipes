The Tacos Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or chicken
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • 8 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • Sour cream, salsa, and guacamole for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or chicken over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the taco seasoning and a little water (as per the seasoning packet instructions). Simmer for 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  4. Assemble the tacos by placing a portion of the cooked meat in each tortilla.
  5. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, chopped onions, and cilantro.
  6. Add a dollop of sour cream, salsa, and guacamole as desired.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Enjoy your homemade tacos!
